# Subject And Communication Routing

Subject routing controls evidence obligations, not palette, font, surface brightness, or a fixed theme. Select the family and communication job semantically from the request, audience, and intended decision. Do not implement raw-prompt keyword or substring classification.

## Subject families and evidence obligations

| Subject family | Evidence obligations |
|---|---|
| Software, interfaces, and developer tools | Show authentic UI, code, traces, states, or execution flow when they support the claim. Do not fabricate screenshots or use decorative code walls. |
| Infrastructure, operations, and security | Show boundaries, authority, dependencies, failure paths, and observable operational evidence. Darkness is optional, not the domain default. |
| Hardware, semiconductors, and manufacturing | Use real equipment, facilities, products, processes, materials, or measured diagrams when available. Show scale and physical constraints. |
| Science, medicine, and research | Use sourced scientific, clinical, laboratory, field, pathology, microscopy, or mechanism evidence as appropriate. Label methods, uncertainty, and factual versus illustrative imagery. |
| Finance, regulation, and trust | Date consequential figures. Separate flows, reserves, claims, assurance, audit, and regulatory status. Keep risk visible beside benefit. |
| Consumer products and services | Show the real product, service moment, package, interface, venue, or user context. Avoid fake product renders and feature-card walls. |
| Games, animation, idols, and entertainment | Prefer official or supplied imagery for real works and people. Preserve person or character identity and distinguish official, fan, and generated material. |
| Travel, places, and hospitality | Use real destination and venue photography, maps, route, location, time, price, and seasonal context when relevant. Never leave image promises as styled placeholders. |
| History, culture, and archives | Date claims, identify primary sources and provenance, and distinguish archive evidence from later interpretation or reconstruction. |
| Organizations, people, and case studies | Ground identities, roles, chronology, decisions, outcomes, and quotes. Avoid generic team stock in place of the actual case. |
| Policy, markets, and social issues | Date datasets and policy status, show affected groups and uncertainty, and distinguish observation, forecast, recommendation, and advocacy. |
| Environment, climate, agriculture, and ecology | Use real field, organism, land, facility, weather, or remote-sensing evidence where appropriate. State scale, location, time, and uncertainty. |

## Communication jobs

| Communication job | Story emphasis |
|---|---|
| Orientation and introduction | Identity, relevance, map of the subject, then a memorable way to continue exploring. |
| Education and explanation | Prior knowledge, one concept at a time, worked example, misconception, and recap. |
| Decision and proposal | Decision context, current cost, options, tradeoffs, recommendation, commitment, and next action. |
| Sales, promotion, and launch | Audience tension, promise, authentic subject reveal, proof, differentiation, offer, and action. |
| Research sharing | Question, method, evidence, uncertainty, finding, implication, and open question. |
| Status and performance report | Target, current state, delta, cause, risk, owner, and next checkpoint. |
| Incident and postmortem | Impact, chronology, contributing conditions, detection, response, learning, and verified corrective action. |
| Roadmap and implementation | Destination, constraints, sequence, dependencies, ownership, gates, and measurable outcomes. |
| Comparison and curation | Selection criteria, comparable evidence, meaningful differences, audience fit, and recommendation. |
| Narrative and showcase | Hook, context, progression, turning point, signature evidence, and a resolved closing image. |

## Combination contract

Record this internal routing before art direction:

```text
Subject family: hardware / semiconductor / manufacturing
Communication job: research sharing
Audience: business leaders + engineering leads
Evidence obligations: real fabs, equipment, package cross-sections, dated capacity data
Design dials: luminosity mixed / density 7 / media need 9 / variance 6 / motion 3
Candidate directions: Editorial Grid / Human Infrastructure / Scientific Atlas
Rejected candidate: Mission Control - the job is market explanation, not live operations
```

Create three theme candidates from subject family x communication job x audience. Subject obligations remain stable across candidates, while typography, color, composition, and motion may differ.
